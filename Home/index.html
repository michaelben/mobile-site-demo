Set ‘upstairs’ on Jersey Rd, Woollahra, the grand travertine staircase leads up to a vibrant cocktail bar and dining haven with a fun, sexy atmosphere and a commitment to excellent food and wine. La Scala on Jersey Restaurant and Cocktail bar exudes sophistication boasting a no fuss Modern Italian menu with a nod to the great classics. The award winning wine list has been carefully developed to match the Modern Italian cuisine at La Scala on Jersey. With over 120 wines by the bottle and over 25 wines by the glass the wine list has been crafted to ensure there is a wine to suit the most discerning palate.

Tony P of SYLVANIA WATERS

We dined here a year ago and it was consistent with the previous visit. The ambience, service and quality of food was excellent. The wine list was expensive - the cheapest bottle was $47. The bill came to $80+ a head with shared entrees and deserts - a little on the expensive side for an average Saturday night but we walked away satisfied and having a wonderful evening. Would go back.
